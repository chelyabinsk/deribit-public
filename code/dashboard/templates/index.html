This project ingeniously merges Julia's computational prowess with Python's presentation abilities to offer critical insights into Deribit's derivatives market, all while upholding the confidentiality of trades and positions.

Features

  • Real-time Derivatives Data
  • Custom Pricing Analysis
  • Visual Market Trends

Integration

By skillfully uniting Julia's computation capabilities and Python's visualization proficiency, the dashboard presents near real-time pricing insights, all without disclosing any trade or position data.

Technologies Used

The Deribit Market Data Dashboard harnesses these technologies:

  • WebSockets: Fetches near real-time market data from Deribit's WebSocket API without revealing any trade or position details.
  • ChartJS: Empowers dynamic charting to illustrate market trends and analysis outcomes.
  • AGGrid: Facilitates interactive data grids, maintaining data privacy.

Dashboard Sections

  1. Fitting and Quoting: Perform advanced pricing calculations and generate quotes for market-making strategies.
    1. Volatility Graph: This page demonstrates Out-of-The-Money (OTM) volatilities and our fair IV (Implied Volatility) fit, offering insight into pricing accuracy and risk assessment.
    2. Quoting Screen: Access real-time prices for all BTC option expirations, along with the model's projected prices. Additionally, monitor your current positions and observe volatility changes over the last 24 hours, enabling comprehensive quoting and strategy execution.
    3. Perpetual Future: Observe historical price trends of BTC PERPETUAL and leverage the plot to potentially identify key support and resistance levels, aiding in strategy formulation and decision-making.
    4. Greeks (of the opened positions): Analyze the Greeks of your open positions to understand the sensitivity of your portfolio to price changes, volatility shifts, and time decay. Utilize this information to refine and optimize your trading strategies.
  2. Trades: Monitor executed trades, ensuring transparency while protecting sensitive trade information.
    1. Public Option Trades: Explore public option trades for insights into market trends and sentiment.
    2. Public Future Trades: Gain visibility into public future trades, providing an overview of the market's sentiment.
    3. Own Option Trades: Analyze your own option trades, helping you make informed decisions about your positions.
    4. Own Future Trades: Observe your own future trades, contributing to a comprehensive understanding of your portfolio.
    5. Own Trades: Consolidated view of your overall trades, assisting in performance analysis and strategy refinement.
  3. Trading Graphs: Visualize market trends and analysis results through dynamic graphs, aiding decision-making.
    1. Implied Probabilities: Gain insights into the market's perception of option probabilities, helping you evaluate potential outcomes.
    2. Zeta Profile: Analyze the sensitivity of option prices to changes in the risk-free rate, aiding in interest rate risk management.
    3. Futures Term Structure: Observe the term structure of futures contracts, offering insights into market expectations over time.
    4. ATM Vol Term Structure: Explore the term structure of at-the-money volatility, providing valuable information about market sentiment and potential price swings.
  4. Admin: Access administrative tools to manage settings and ensure seamless dashboard functionality.

Important Notice: This is a public-facing dashboard designed to showcase specific functionalities. Certain sections related to sensitive trading and proprietary information have been intentionally disabled to ensure data privacy and security for public viewers.
